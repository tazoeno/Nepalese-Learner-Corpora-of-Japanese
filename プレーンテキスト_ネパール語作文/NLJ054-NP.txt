Ekjana kta manxe rukh ma chadhera fruits tipdaii thyo. Rukh bata orliyera fruits Haru basket ma lagayera feri rukh ma chadhyo . Tyaspaxi basket ko chheu bata auta bakhro ra ekjana manxe basket agadi bata uta gayo. Ekxin paxi auta kta cycle ma ayo ra basket heryo ra auta chorera cycle ma halera bhagyo. Bato ma jadai garda auta kti sanga vet vayo ra kta ko topi pani hawa le Idaho ra Ali para gayesi dhunga ma cycle thokiyera kta ladyo ra fruits jharera chharapasta bhayo. Kta ladeko dekhera 3 Jana kta haru le aayera uthayo ra pathayo. Ali partira gayesi hawa le udako topi auta kta le lyayera diyo. Pachhi 3 Jana le pani fruits khadai gayo . Rukh ma chadeko manxe tala orliyera herda auta basket nai thyena ra xakka paryo . Uta bata 3 Jana kta le fruits khadai aayo dekhera ra xakka paryo . 3 Jana kta haru tya bata katera gayo fruits tipne manxe hereko herai vayo
