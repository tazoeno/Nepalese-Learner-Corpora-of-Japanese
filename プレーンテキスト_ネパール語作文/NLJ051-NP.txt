Euta manchhe rukh ma chadera fal tipdai thiyo. tes pachhi teo scene hatera feri arko thau ma dui jana manxe bakhra liyera aaudai thiye. teha euta manchhe le rukh ma chadera fal tipdai thiyo. fal tipera tala jhola ma jamma gardai thiyo. tes pachhi ek jana sano bacchha aayera teha tala rakheko fal aafno cycle ma rakhera liyera gayo. liyera jadai garda euta keti agadi bata aayeko bela teo keti herne bela ma cycle agadi dhunga ma thokiyera cycle ladera cycle  ma rakheko fal sabai vuima pokhiyo.teo pokhiyeko bela teo thau ma vayeko aru 3 jana bacchha le teo ladeko bachha lai dekhera aayera uthayera vuima pokhiyeko fal pani tipera cycle mathi rakhdiyo.tes paxi teo bacchha cycle chadera feri gayo. tara usle aafno topi lagna birseko vayeko le teha ko 3 jana bacchha le topi liyera aaidiye. tespachhi teo baccha le baaki 3 jana bacchha lai aafu sanga vayeko fal haru khana lai diyo. tini haru fal khadai paile teo fal tipdai gareko manchhe ko agadi bata jadai garda teo manchhe le dekhyo.sayed teo manchhe le teo 3 jana bacchha le aafno fal chorera khadai chha vanne maile aafule soche. dhanyawaad
