Yeuta manxy rukma chadya rw naspati tipdhi thiyo. Tetibela arko manxy ley bakra tanyarw ruk ko najikai batw liya rw gayako thiyo. Yek xinma yeuta kta cycle ma aaunxa rw uley tyavayako naspati manxy lai khi navani leya rw janxa. Bato ma jadhi gardha pari batw yeuta kt cycle ma aaunxa . Tyo kt lai herdha cycle palthinxa . Najikai vaya ko kta haru ley uthau na sahayog garxa. Sahayog garya ki vaya rw kta ley naspati dhiya re padhunxa . Rw tyo kta haru ruk ko najikai batw janxa rw tyo manxy herya rw chak parxa .
