Yauta bajya rukh ma chadyara naspati tipdai yauta bhada ma rakhnu hunxa ane tesai ma yauta kto cycle chadyara aauxa ane yata uta harxa ane tyo bhadako naspati aafno cycle ko toraki ma rahara janxa ane bato ma yauta kti dakhxa kti lai hardai jada yauta thulo dhunga ma aaljyara ladxa  ane aagi chorara layako naspati sab pokhenxa tesai ma bato ma khaldai garako 3 jana bachha haru la dakhxa ane sahayog garna aauxa . Bachha haru la tyo kta ko naspati ane cycle ane topi uthayara tai kta lai denxa. Ane thyo kta la tyo bachha haru lai yauta yauta naspati denxa ane tyo bachha haru la pani la na dhoi kana luga ma poxyara khadai aafno bato janxan. Tesai ma bajya rukh bata jhanu hunxa ane aafula batulako naspati ko bada harnu hunxa tara tyo bhada rakhako thau ma huna bajya chhaka parara basnu hunxa ane tesai ma tai bachha haru naspati khadai aako dakhnu hunxa ane harako harai parnu hunxa.
