Aauta manis ruk ma falful tipdai thiyo ani tala aauta bata ma halera xode ko thiyo. Aauta kt chai bakhara leyara aayo ko thiyo tyo bato ma. Para bata aauta baccha saikal ma aairakhe ko thiyo ani tesle tala rakhe ko falful dekhyo ani aauta jhola saikal ko aagadi halera gayo. Kehe tada puge paxi aarko patte aauta kt lai deyo tyo karam ma usko tauko ma laga ko topi hawa ko karan le tala jharyo . Ani usko saikal ko ladbadena thalyo ani dhunga ma gayera thokiyo. Tyo thau ma tin jana aaru manxe base rakhe ko thiye ani uni haru le tyo baccha lai sahayog gare. Tate bela samma tini haru lai tyo baccha chor ho bhanne thaya thiye na. Tyo baccha tya bata utheyo ani saikal doraudai aafno bato lage tara yeta feri tyo ruk ma chade ko manxe bhane tala jhardai thiyo usle aauta jhola gayeb vaye ko thaya payo ani usle khojyo payena. Tate kai ma tyo bato tye tin jana manxe aaye ani tyo falful sau le tyo manxe haru lai heryo ani sakiyo
